Welcome to our beehive!

We understand the challenges of starting over in a new country and we want to help you.

Our main goal is to empower female, transgender, and non-binary immigrants in Sweden to break into the technology industry by providing a support network and help with their career transition.

By doing this, we also aim to increase diversity and inclusion at the working enviroment.
